# About Me

Hey there! I am currently a graduate student in the <a href="https://codesign.berkeley.edu/">Cognition and Computation in Design (Co-Design) Lab</a> at UC Berkeley, advised by <a href="http://www.kosagl.com/">Kosa Goucher-Lambert</a>.  

My current interests include (but are not limited to)
- Physical-digital modeling/twinning for early-stage design 
- Human-machine collaboration for engineering design
- Sustainability in product design
- Universal design

I graduated with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from MIT in June 2019, where I worked on projects ranging from assistive technology to stretchable electronics, before moving back to the Bay Area.

Outside of research and work, you can find me reading, running, or trying out new recipes.
In addition, I am a former competitive figure skater, a beginner guitar player, and a coffee lover!
