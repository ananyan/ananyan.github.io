I originally added the blog section of this website because of what I expected this website to be – a place to let whoever stumbled upon it know what I was contributing to the world. I hadn’t quite figured out whether I wanted to have a fancy portfolio site or a more simple academic homepage (still haven’t) … but I’ve always had a lot of thoughts in my head that I tend to keep to myself (which may or may not be a good thing). I initially thought that these thoughts could be part of my contribution throughout and after graduate school. Then I deleted the blog section because, to be honest, it’s pretty scary to put your opinions out on the web, regardless of if anyone ever reads it or not. Furthermore, I’ve been reading a lot about data, privacy, and ethics which has played a part in how torn I am between those considerations and web presence. Well, having a shelter-in-place order in the Bay Area for the next 4 weeks has given me the opportunity to think a lot – too much – and has led me to the  conclusion that it’s a good chance to try to add the section back. It’s an unusual time, but maybe something good can come of it!
